Welcome to Excellence in Cardiac Care

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Alexander Thorne is a renowned cardiologist dedicated to providing compassionate, patient-centered care. He believes in a holistic approach to heart health, combining cutting-edge medical technology with personalized treatment plans.

Dr. Thorne completed his medical degree at Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine and his residency and fellowship in Cardiology at the Mayo Clinic. He is a fellow of the American College of Cardiology and regularly contributes to cardiology research.

Our Services

Comprehensive cardiac care tailored to your unique needs

Preventive Cardiology

Proactive heart health strategies to prevent cardiovascular disease before it starts. Comprehensive risk assessments and lifestyle guidance.

Heart Disease Management

Expert treatment for coronary artery disease, heart failure, and other cardiac conditions with personalized care plans.

Cardiac Testing

State-of-the-art diagnostic testing including ECG, echocardiograms, stress tests, and advanced imaging technologies.

Consultations

Thorough evaluations and second opinions for complex cardiac conditions with clear, understandable explanations.

Hypertension Management

A dedicated program to accurately diagnose, monitor, and effectively control high blood pressure through tailored medication, reducing your risk of future complications.

Cholesterol & Lipid Disorder Clinic

Specialized focus on diagnosing and treating complex cholesterol and triglyceride issues through advanced medication management to improve your cardiovascular health.
